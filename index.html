What I am doing now

I’m currently in high school, trying to get by a flawed system - to me, at least. I really dislike a lot of people around me and those I see online. It’s frustrating to see all these people have these strange habits and beliefs, some can’t be helped, but for those who stick by these horrid things, it seems rather unbelievable. I almost feel like I look for impurities and imperfections in everyone and everything, but I don’t want to be that way. I want to focus on learning and the positive side of life and to strive to work towards something better than what I’ve got going on now. So many things, happening all at once.

Plans for my Future

To be honest, I really have no idea what I’m doing. I have no clue if I want to go to collage, or move to another country, or just gamble my life away. My dream profession is to fly planes, commercially, privately, or even for cargo. I really want to pursue this career, but as a learn more and more about pilots and how they typically live their lives, its usually really hard for them to spend time with their loved ones or their friends, something I value a lot. I struggle to decide what I want to do in my immediate future, and what to do for the rest of my life. Having all these absurdly important decisions made in so short time is...really tough, especially for a high school student.
